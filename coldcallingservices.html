Inspiring Prospects to Convert


ExpertCallers’ offshore cold calling services is designed to help you connect with your customers in the most productive way. With over 12 years of experience in providing cold calling outsourcing services for companies across industries, we have mastered this art in more ways than one.


For us, cold calling is more about compelling than pushing. We believe in delivering messages through engagements. It doesn’t matter what industry you are into, if you have something of value to offer, we create engrossing engagements to inspire the buyer to convert.


Our cold calling outsourcing services can help you with the following:


Define your markets


In case of companies which are starting out and existing ones who want to realign their objectives and goals, it’s important to be updated on the needs of your market. Cold calling helps you gather a vast amount of feedback from potential and non-potential clients that can give you an airtight idea about your target market. You can customise other marketing activities accurately based on your market and achieve better results.


Investigate a company's business and their human resources needs


Most often, the information that can’t be found on websites can be gathered just by asking. To understand the pain points of your customers, sometimes all it takes is a phone call. By not making every phone call about sales, but rather focusing on listening to understand your customer’s business and how to help them better can increase trust for your company among your customers.


Carry out lead generations to capture prospects


Employing cold calling services is a far better way to capture prospects than spending tons on newspaper ads or Google ads. Not just that - if you outsource cold calling you can also get access to lead generation reports and business intelligence, based on which your sales team can make follow up calls. The end-to-end solution from lead verification to lead progression to lead nurturing can help you get quality leads to ensure you can target the right prospects and avoid wasting precious marketing resources.


Conduct email campaigns


Not everyone can send effective cold emails that won’t be discarded as spam. We are one of the best cold calling companies who have perfected the art of effective cold emails that actually bring you conversions. We don’t believe in an automated system that sends hundreds of run-of-the-mill emails but rather personalise each campaign to suit your marketing needs and your customer requirements. We also help with monitoring and analysing the results of the cold emails to identify what works best for your company.


Craft good calling scripts


One of the most indispensable tools that ensure cold calling success is a good cold calling script. Our cold calling services include preparing cold calling scripts that can serve as reference points for future sales conversations. Prepared with thorough research and backed by data, the script can ensure that callers are well prepared to handle different scenarios and personalise conversations based on the various stages of the funnel. It can help you understand your audience effectively and continue the conversation to gain more conversions.


Send out useful introductory notices


Let’s face it. Most often, brand communication gets lost in a flurry of other important activities. At the end of the day, this offers an inconsistent picture of your company in the minds of your customers. Most of the time, it’s not possible to send out regular brand communication to all your customers. Once you outsource cold calling for your brand, it becomes easier to send out important notices and announcements regularly with all your brand guidelines in place. It helps create a seamless customer experience as well as keeps them in the know of all important updates.

Our Approach to Cold Calling Activities


Our cold calling techniques are based on the following foundational principles.


Part 1: Identifying the product


In the first part, we focus on the supplier or product or the service that is to be represented. We choose to represent products/services that fit a specific purpose for a particular market or application. This helps make our opening calls stronger.


Once the product that is to be represented is decided, our next step is to ensure that the organisation we are representing is ethical, socially responsible and conforms with the expectations of its target market. If it fails to meet any of the criteria, as your representative, our team of callers will not have strong foundations/propositions to build-on and market the product or service in a convincing way. In this case, we would engage in a discussion to suggest an alternative product or service to represent.


Step 2: Stepping into the customer’s shoes


Once a product or service is identified, our second step is preparing for the call. The Cold calling service providers at ExpertCallers view this activity as strategic and empowering. While less experienced outsourcing providers think of each call as a random and impersonal numbers game, we understand the power of communication and persuasion and embrace it.


Step 3: Nailing the call


Our third part is understanding the product offering. At Expert Callers, we try our best to understand your business from the ground up to arm ourselves adequately before picking up the phone. One fundamental quality that we possess that significantly improves the effectiveness of our cold calling services is that we have a good strategic perception of the issues faced by the prospect and deliver an apt opening proposition.
